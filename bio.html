About Me

I am currently a junior at Tufts University majoring in biology and minoring in computer science. I am interested in the intersection between healthcare and technology, and I hope to use technology to help improve health outcomes, especially in underserved communities. I am also interested in neuroscience, and much of my research focuses on the pathophysiology of neurodegenerative diseases. At Tufts, I am the co-president of Tufts NeuroNetwork, an organization focused on cultivating a community interested in neuroscience on the undergraduate campus, as well as a member of Tufts Premedical Society, and the Ballroom Team. In my free time, I enjoy learning new languages, traveling, baking banana bread, and biking along The Charles!
